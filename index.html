What Is The Cats?.

The CaTs (Collaborating & Technology) club, established in November 2023 at Chungbuk National University's Department of Computer Science, emphasizes practical experience through real development and product/service creation. With a focus on collaboration, the club fosters organic communication among its members and engages in various projects

Mobile

SERVER

We practice loading AI models, creating endpoints, and utilizing Docker and Kubernetes using frameworks like Django and Spring

UNITY

Our goal is to foster a collaborative environment where club members participate in various game projects freely, with the ultimate aim of distributing the completed games

CHATBOT

We provide convenient features for CATS members through the development of a Kakao Chatbot

SEMINAR
