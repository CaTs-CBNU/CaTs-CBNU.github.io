What Is The CaTs?.

The CaTs (Collaborating & Technology) club, established in November 2023 at Chungbuk National University's Department of Computer Engineering, emphasizes practical experience through real development and product/service creation. With a focus on collaboration, the club fosters organic communication among its members and engages in various projects

  • Developing in a free atmosphere.
  • Through hands-on learning, grasping concepts beyond what theory alone can teach.
  • Gaining an understanding of the operational principles by directly experimenting across various aspects of computing.
  • In particular, with mobile development, one can experience learning both Android and iOS through various languages such as Java, Kotlin, Swift, and Flutter.
